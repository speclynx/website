Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Our Products

SpecLynx products (OpenAPI Toolkit, Language Service, ApiDOM) run entirely on your machine. They do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Your API specifications never leave your device.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address to send you product updates. We use LaunchList to manage subscriptions. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Website

This website does not use cookies or tracking scripts. We do not collect analytics data.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at info@speclynx.com.
